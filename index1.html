Our Story

The Magic is in the Details

Since 1993, Letona Cafe has been crafting meals that bring people together. Born in Yakkala and now thriving in Ja-Ela too, we blend Sri Lankan soul with global flavours — from devilled seafood and kottu to hand-stretched pizzas and herb-crusted lamb chops.

Fresh ingredients, passionate chefs, and a team that genuinely cares. That's what puts a smile on every face that walks through our doors.
